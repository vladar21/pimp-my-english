Rules

1. Press Start button to begin the quiz.

2. Press Settings link to make quiz more controllable. In this section you can select:
total number of words for quiz;
cefr levels of words for quiz ('A1', 'B1', 'B2' etc);
types of words for quiz ('noun', 'adjective' etc);

3. On the current quiz screen, you can see the following elements:
at the top, the definition of the word to be guessed;
below, the image as a clue that may help you guess the correct answer;
below, the word variants for the answer, with audio pronunciation support;
below, the 30-second countdown timer for answering. If you answer later - the quiz will automatically stop;
on the right, the Stop quiz button;
on the left, the Next question button;
in the bottom left corner, the number of quiz questions already answered and the total number of questions;
in the bottom right corner, the number of correct and incorrect answers in the current attempt.

4. After pressing Stop button, you will be taken to the winners table, where you can see the results of the current attempt and all previous attempts.
