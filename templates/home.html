Welcome to Pimp My English !


Enhance your English skills with our engaging quizzes and personalized learning tools. Pimp My English is designed to make learning fun and effective for everyone, whether you are a beginner or an advanced learner.


User Roles and Subscription Plans


Subscribers

Subscribers have access to all platform features, such as creating and editing word sets, through paid subscriptions. Enjoy personalized learning experiences!

Guests

Guests can use the service without registration or authorization, limited to accessing preset and shared word sets. Upgrade to a subscription to unlock all features.

Subscription Plans

There are two subscription plans, a cheap one and a very cheap one: €10 per month or €50 per year.

Easy Subscription Management

Manage your subscription with ease in just one click.


Features


Interactive Quizzes

Our quizzes are designed to test your knowledge and help you improve your English in an interactive and enjoyable way. Each quiz is tailored to different proficiency levels and covers a wide range of topics.

Vocabulary Building

Expand your vocabulary with our curated word lists and exercises. Our vocabulary building tools are designed to help you learn new words and use them effectively in different contexts.

Custom Word Sets

As a Subscriber, you can create personalized English word sets using our unique Quiz Maker tools. Tailor your learning experience to your specific needs and interests.

Ready-Made Word Sets

As a Guest save time by using our pre-made English word sets on various topics. These sets are ready to use and can help you quickly expand your vocabulary.
